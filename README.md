## 🎯 What This Project Is About

This **Power BI Sales Dashboard** was built to transform raw transaction and customer data into a clear, interactive story for business decision-makers. It covers **2 years of sales activity** across multiple countries and product categories — giving instant answers to the questions that matter most:

- 📦 Which products are driving revenue?
- 🌍 Where are our customers located?
- 📅 When do sales peak — and why?
- 🛍️ Which category dominates the market?

## 💡 Key Business Insights

> 🔍 Findings from the dashboard analysis

- 🚀 **Dell XPS 15 is the clear revenue champion** — generating $24.5K alone, nearly 24% of total sales
- 📱 **Smartphones dominate by category** — accounting for over 51% of total category revenue
- 🇺🇸 **8 out of 12 customers are US-based** — strong home market with room to grow internationally
- 📅 **Revenue peaks in mid-to-late 2026** — seasonal demand signals ideal for planning marketing campaigns
- 🎯 **Top 3 customers drive outsized revenue** — high-value retention strategy is critical
